Welcome!

Digital Image Media Lab. was founded at Yonsei University in 1996. Under supervision of
Prof. Kwanghoon Sohn, various topics in digital image processing field have been researched.

Specific topics we are interested in and researching currently are:

  • Digital Image processing

    • 3D Video Acquisition
    Multi-view Video Processing
    Image Enhancement / Editing

  • Intelligent Computer Vision

    • ADAS(advanced driver assistance systems)
    Multispectral Imaging / Feature Extraction
    Deep Learning for Vision
