Selected Publication

    In computer vision, the top tier conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, AAAI) have greater impact than most SCI journals. Oral presentations have an highly competitive acceptance rate of about 4% and poster presentations about 20%. According to the Google Scholar statistics, their H5-index are 240 for CVPR, 169 for NeurIPS, 129 for ICCV, 137 for ECCV, and 95 for AAAI, which correspond to the top 1 - 5 rank of all computer science journals and conferences. Note that CVPR is the only conference proceedings listed in the top 10 publications in Google Scholar and is ranked No.1 in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. IEEE TPAMI and TIP have among the highest JCR impact factors across all computer science categories, such as 17.73 for TPAMI (top 0.4%) and 6.79 (top 6.8%) for TIP.
